About Art Appliance LLC

Jacksonville's trusted appliance repair professionals since 2018.

Who We Are

Art Appliance LLC is a locally owned and operated appliance repair company serving the Jacksonville, Florida metro area. Founded with a commitment to honest, reliable service, we've built our reputation on getting the job done right the first time — every time.

Our team brings over 7 years of combined experience to every repair. We specialize in residential appliance services, working on all major brands.

Our Mission

We believe that appliance repair shouldn't be stressful. That's why we've built our business around transparency, speed, and quality. From the moment you call us, you'll experience clear communication, upfront pricing, and professional service that puts your needs first.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Same-day service available — call before noon for priority scheduling
  • Experienced technicians on all major brands
  • Upfront pricing with no hidden fees or surprise charges
  • Locally owned and operated — we're your neighbors

Residential Expertise

Whether you need a quick fix for your refrigerator, oven, or microwave, we have the expertise and equipment to handle it. Our services cover refrigerators, ovens, microwaves, dishwashers, washers, dryers, and more.
