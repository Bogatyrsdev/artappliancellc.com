Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day appliance repair?

Yes! We offer same-day service for most residential and commercial appliance repairs. Call us before noon for the best chance of same-day availability.

How much does a diagnostic visit cost?

Our flat diagnostic fee is $97 for residential appliances and $197 for commercial equipment. This fee is applied toward the repair cost if you choose to proceed.

Do you repair high-end and luxury appliances?

Absolutely. Our technicians are factory-trained and certified to work on Sub-Zero, Wolf, Viking, Thermador, Miele, and other premium brands.

What warranty do you offer on repairs?

We provide a 1-year warranty on all labour and a 6-month warranty on original replacement parts, giving you peace of mind after every repair.

Are your technicians certified?

Yes, all our technicians are factory-trained and hold certifications from major appliance manufacturers. They undergo regular training to stay current.

Do you service commercial restaurant equipment?

Yes, we service walk-in coolers, ice machines, commercial refrigerators, fryers, ovens, and kitchen ventilation systems.
